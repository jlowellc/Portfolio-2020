Gingerbread House

This is the first MVP site I delivered to a potential customer when I began freelancing.

This site was all about delivering an MVP, and it is about as simple as you can get. The color scheme is not thought out, and the site still uses dummy text. The customer didn't really know what they wanted, so I delivered a blank page so to speak. After the MVP was delivered, converstaions concerning the design were had; however, the customer decided to stay with the Facebook page. Concerns over privacy were the biggest obstacle. Because this was a page for a daycare, there were significant worries over child privacy and usage concerns. Because of these concerns, the project was abandoned.
